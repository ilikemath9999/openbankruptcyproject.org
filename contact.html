For Researchers

If you are an academic researcher studying consumer bankruptcy outcomes and would like access to our dataset, methodology, or replication materials, please email us with your institutional affiliation and research area. We are happy to collaborate.

For Journalists

We are available for interviews, background briefings, and data requests. We can provide district-level statistics, attorney performance data, and context on federal bankruptcy court operations. See our press page for key facts and figures.
